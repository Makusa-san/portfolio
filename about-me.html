About Me

Hey, I'm Max — a UI designer and front-end developer based in Vancouver, currently studying at SFU. I'm interested in turning ideas into experiences that feel natural, structured, and alive, where users can move through a website almost like a story.

I am seeking opportunities in UI/UX design and front-end development that allow me to apply my skills in creating intuitive and visually coherent digital interfaces. I am particularly interested in roles that emphasize user-centered design, collaboration, and continuous learning.
